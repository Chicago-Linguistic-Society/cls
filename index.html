About

The Chicago Linguistic Society is the oldest student-run linguistics organization in the United States. Our linguistics conference has been held every year since 1965. This academic year, CLS will host its 52nd annual meeting, which will be held from Thursday April 21, to Sunday, April 23, 2016.
