About

The Chicago Linguistic Society is the oldest student-run linguistics organization in the United States. Our linguistics conference has been held every year since 1965. This academic year, CLS will host its 55th annual meeting, which will be held from Thursday, May 16, to Saturday, May 18, 2019.
