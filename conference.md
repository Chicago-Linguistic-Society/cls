# Registration

Registration is now open for CLS 59! If you plan to attend, please fill out <a href="https://forms.gle/3HRJhQF2D8Kv8bmYA">this form</a> and pay your registration fee at <a href="https://paypal.me/CLS59Reg">this link</a>. Registration rates are as follows:
- UChicago Students - FREE
- Non-UChicago Students - $40 USD
- Recent PhDs - $80 USD
- Faculty/Others - $120 USD

If you are attending the Saturday night banquet, you should add $20 USD to your total. 

NOTE: All attendees, whether presenting research or not, are required to fill out the registration form. Also note that this year's conference will be **in-person only**, so please do not fill out the form if you will not be able to come to Chicago in April.
